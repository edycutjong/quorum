NORTHWIND INDUSTRIES — GOVERNANCE CHARTER (Excerpt)

Section 4.2 — Financial Authorization Levels

All expenditures must follow the dual-authorization protocol:

Tier 1 (< $50K): Department Manager + Finance Analyst
Tier 2 ($50K - $500K): Director + VP Finance
Tier 3 ($500K - $1M): VP + CFO (written co-sign required)
Tier 4 (> $1M): VP + CFO + Board Resolution (written, filed with Corporate Secretary)

Deviations from this protocol require:
1. Written exception memo from CEO
2. Post-hoc ratification by the Audit Committee within 30 days
3. Documentation filed with General Counsel

Section 4.3 — Vendor Onboarding

New vendors exceeding $100K annual contract value must:
1. Complete vendor due diligence questionnaire
2. Provide certificate of insurance
3. Execute standard MSA (Master Service Agreement)
4. File Statement of Work (SOW) with clear deliverables and milestones
5. Receive approval from Procurement Committee

Non-compliance with Section 4.2 or 4.3 constitutes a governance breach
subject to investigation by the Audit Committee.

Source: Northwind Industries Governance Charter, Rev. 12 (January 2024)
